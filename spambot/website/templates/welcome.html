Welcome to the warm spam project, part of the Digital Sizzle 2012 hackathon.

This project has two parts. A website, that collects recordings of people reading spam messages, and a radio station, where those recordings are made into something beautiful by combining them with an algorithmic composition by the guys at Reactify

If you would like to participate, and have you're voice heard saying potentially naughty things in the Whitechapel Gallery on September 26th, you will need a phone ( we'll call you to record the spam message ) and a seperate screen from which to read the piece you're going to record.


If you're ready, lets get started!
